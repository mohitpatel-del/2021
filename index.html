Date - 1 May 2021,

Quarantine, I should double check that spelling, Ya so during lockdown I've been reading quite a lot of books. And this is basically an overview of my experience with them. 2021 is going great, plan on reading 20 books this year.

  • Ego is the enemy- Ryan Holiday

A great book, A book I would recommend to everyone. As the name says book is about ego, it is kinda hard to read because of all the self-evaluation we do as we go through this book.

  • A subtle art of not giving a fu*k- Mark Manson

See we care a lot about what other people think of us, we care about a lot of trifling matters which are pointless, this book teaches about what to worry about and what shoud we care about.

  • Kafka on the shore- Haruki Murakami

Imagine you are riding a bike, its evening you are seeing a wonderful sunset, the wind is hitting your face and you just feel utmost peace. That feeling is this book, go read it.

  • Norweigian wood- Haruki Murakami

It is really funny book, and it surely will make you cry a fictional emotional rollercoster.

  • Switch- Dan and Chip Heath

Change is hard right, this book will make it easy. I did change a lot in this quarantine thanks to this book.

  • Everything is fu*ked:A book about hope- Mark manson

Don't read this book it is not for you, just maintain a distance from this book. Just don't okay.

  • Stillness is the key- Ryan Holiday

We often loose control, anger, anxiety takes the best of us, sometimes pressure it just gets hard to breath in some situations, this books wisdom will help you maintain stillness when you need it the most.
